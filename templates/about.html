About Us

Welcome to Insight Extractor. We believe that reading long, tedious documents shouldn't take hours of your day. We built this tool to help researchers, students, and professionals extract deep insights instantly.

Powered by state-of-the-art AI technology from Google Gemini, Insight Extractor breaks down complicated PDFs, Word Docs, and text files into digestible pieces of information like Context, Methodology, Key Features, Advantages, and Disadvantages.

Not only can you summarize documents, but our interactive ecosystem lets you chat with your documents naturally, and automatically generate quizzes to test your knowledge retention.
